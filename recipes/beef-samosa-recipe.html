Beef Samosa

Description

Kenyan beef samosas are delicious, crispy pastries filled with a spiced ground beef mixture. They are a popular street food and snack in Kenya, often enjoyed at parties or during special occasions. The samosas are typically triangular in shape and are deep-fried to golden perfection, making them a delightful blend of crunchy exterior and savory filling. Served hot with a tangy dipping sauce, they're perfect for sharing with friends and family!

Ingredients

For the Filling

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 medium onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tsp ginger, minced
  • 1-2 green chillies, finely chopped (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp tumeric powder
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

For the dough

  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil or ghee
  • Water, as needed

For Frying

  • Oil for deep frying

Steps

  1. Prepare the filling
    • In a large skillet, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat
    • Saut√© the chopped onions until translucent, then add garlic, ginger, and green chillies for another minute
    • Add the ground beef, cooking until browned. Stir in the spices and cilantro, then let cool
  2. Make the dough
    • Combine flour salt in a bowl, adding oil until crumbly. Gradually mix in water until you have a smooth dough. Let it rest for 30 minutes
  3. Assemble the samosas
    • Divide the dough into small balls and roll each into a thin circle. Cut in half to form cones, fill with beef mixture, and seal the edges.
  4. Fry the samosas
    • Heat oil in a deep pan and fry the samosas in batches until golden brown. Drain on paper towels
  5. Serve
    • Enjoy hot with a dipping sauce of your choice
