Mandazi Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp ground cardamom (or cinnamon)
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk (or regular milk)
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tsp melted butter

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, and ground cardamom. In another bowl, whisk together the coconut milk, egg, and melted butter
  2. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix until a dough forms. Knead on a lighly floured surface for about 5 minutes until smooth.
  3. Cover the dough with a clean cloth and let it rest for about 30 minutes
  4. Roll out he dough to about 1/4 inch thickness. Cut into triangles or circles using a knife o cookie cutter.
  5. Heat oil in a deep par over medium heat. Once hot, carefully add the dough pieces in batches, frying until golden brown on both sides (about 3-4 minutes). Drain on paper towels.
